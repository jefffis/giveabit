Giveab.it

What is Giveab.it?

Most of us, at least those who’ll be visiting sites like this often, have a few bucks here and there to spare. And the rest of us need those few bucks every now and again. Some of us need it way more than others. Rather than buying another coffee or another drink, or if you find yourself with a few dollars extra at the end of the month, why not share it with those who truly need it?

Giveab.it is a test project that I’m working on trying to build a simple way to donate money to charities that need it. In our busy world we often forget that us in the tech industry have it pretty sweet, so I want to make something that’s also sweet but can do a lot of good for others.
